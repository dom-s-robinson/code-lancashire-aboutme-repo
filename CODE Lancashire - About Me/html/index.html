About Me

Target-driven and highly motivated Business Development professional comfortable pitching to c-level SME business leaders and discussing a range of complex and sensitive topics. Adaptable across commercial business and academia, influencing stakeholders at every level.

I currently work within the partnership and engagement team at Lancaster university who are responsible for developing a suite of training and development programmes for SME business leaders to stimulate growth and innovation in the local economy. Predominantly responsible for the long-standing and high-performing programme ‘The Lancashire Forum’ but working across our full range of programmes to ensure the correct channel for our clients. My main duties include developing relationships with SME c-level prospective clients, conducting business diagnostic meetings to understand their needs and how we can help, handling both inbound enquiries and generating my own leads through desk-research, social media, direct-mail, networking events and expos, engaging with and building relationships with other stakeholders such as local chambers of commerce, Local Enterprise Partnerships, private networking communities and being the face of Lancaster University Management Schools business engagement activities. Assisting with effective marketing and communications and event planning. Maintaining effective records of both prospective leads, current clients, and all communications through a centeralised CRM system (salesforce) and running reports to track progress and generate lead lists. Input on design of programmes to meet the needs of the business community. Maintaining relationships with client’s post-programme delivery to keep them engaged with the university, signposting to other relevant programmes or academic research opportunities and ensuring they continue their relationship with the university.
